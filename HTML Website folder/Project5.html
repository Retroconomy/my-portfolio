About This Project

Rooted Realms is a strategic board game where players embody trees competing to spread their roots across the field and absorb vital resources such as nutrients, water, and minerals. To win, players must balance strategic growth, resource management, and overcoming challenges such as rocks, pests, and competing roots.

This game is designed for players who enjoy a mix of strategy and resource management. With each turn, decisions must be made about which path to pursue and how to allocate resources effectively. The winner is the player who reaches a nutrient threshold or dominates the field by covering the most ground!

Key Features

  • Dynamic Gameplay: Compete with other players for limited resources and navigate through obstacles.
  • Beautiful Components: Vibrant game boards, tokens, and player pieces add an engaging visual experience.
  • Replayability: The randomized resource placement ensures a unique game every time.
  • Accessible Mechanics: Easy-to-learn gameplay with strategic depth.
