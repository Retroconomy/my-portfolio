About This Portfolio

This portfolio was developed as part of my Visual Art and Design Module. The aim of this project was to explore game concept designs and refine my ability to present cohesive ideas in an engaging and creative format. From early sketches to finalized concepts, this portfolio reflects the thought process, creativity, and technical skills involved in crafting immersive game ideas.

The slideshow below showcases the culmination of this module. It includes design inspiration, gameplay mechanics, and key visual elements that bring these concepts to life. By using structured layouts and vivid imagery, I have aimed to create a portfolio that effectively communicates my creative vision and technical approach to game design.
